Macaroni and Cheese

This dish is a Homemade delight full of nostalgia and good tastes.

A filling and creamy dish your kids will be wanting every night of the week

Ingredients

  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Cream of Chicken
  • Macaroni
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Directions

  1. First boil your water with salt added
  2. Once the water boild, add the dry Macaroni in. Turn the heat to low and put the lid on.
  3. After abpout 8 minutes, the Macaroni should be cooked. Drain the water
  4. Add Butter, Cream of Chicken, Salt and Pepper, and Milk.
  5. Enjoy!
