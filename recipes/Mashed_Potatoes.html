Mashed Potatoes

This recipe shows you how to mkake the most cremiest mashed potatoes.

You will love this recipe that has been handed down through generations.

Ingredients

  • Russet Potatoes
  • Butter
  • salt
  • Pepper
  • Milk
  • Parmasean

Directions

  1. First Boil the water with salt
  2. while your water is boiling, cut and wash your potatoes into quarters
  3. once your water is boiling, put your patoes in the pot
  4. let your potatoes cook, once you are able to poke them with a fork and it goes in easy, then they are done.
  5. once the potatoes are done, put the butter in and put a lid on the pot for the buitter to melt
  6. Take the lid off and put in the milk.
  7. SMASH the potatoes!!!
  8. if potatoes need more milk add more milk and add parm if desired.
  9. Enjoy!!
