Bannana Bread

This bannana bread is sooo good when it's hot with melted butter on top.

You will enjoy this recipe that has been handed down through generations.

(This is a Triple batch with 9 Bannanas)

Ingredients

  • 6 1/2 cups flour
  • 3 cups sugar
  • 9 1/2 teasp. Baking Powder
  • 3 teasp. salt
  • 9 tlbsp. Salad Oil
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 9 Bannanas
  • nuts

Directions

  1. Put all this in a bowl and beat
  2. Pour into bread pans
  3. Bake at 350 Degrees for 55 to 60 minutes
  4. Enjoy!!
